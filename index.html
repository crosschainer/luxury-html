Your Voice, Your Impact:

Luxuro's governance system puts the decision-making process directly in the hands of our users. Your insights, suggestions, and ideas will shape the future of the platform, ensuring that it evolves in a direction that truly resonates with your needs and aspirations.

As a Luxuro governance participant, you'll have the opportunity to propose and vote on key changes, upgrades, and additions to the platform. When Luxuro's governance feature is live, you'll be able to stake our native token to gain voting power. This voting power translates to your ability to participate in shaping Luxuro's future, from choosing new assets to be listed to proposing changes in platform fees and beyond.
